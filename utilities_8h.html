template<typename TYPE >
void smart_delete ( SmartPointer< TYPE > &  sp)

SmartPointers are usually not used to point to objects created with new. However, sometimes this is useful. The distruction of a SmartPointer requires to split the step in two parts. This little utility does precisely this.

Deprecated:
SmartPointers have been supersed by std_cxx11::shared_ptr, which takes care of destruction as well.
